Sanskrit Shlokas संस्कृत श्लोक

SHLOKA I
गंगे च यमुने चैव गोदावरि सरस्वति।
नर्मदे सिन्धु कावेरी जलेऽस्मिन् सन्निधिं कुरु॥
Invocation of all sacred rivers. O Ganga, Yamuna, Godavari, Saraswati, Narmada, Sindhu, and Kaveri — please be present in this holy water.
SHLOKA II
देवि सुरेश्वरि भगवति गंगे
त्रिभुवन तारिणि तरल तरंगे।
शङ्करमौलिविहारिणि विमले
मम मतिरास्तां तव पदकमले॥
Prayer to Maa Ganga seeking blessings and purification. O divine Ganga, purifier of the three worlds, let my mind rest at your lotus feet.
SHLOKA III
पतितोद्धारिणि गंगे
खण्डित गिरिवर मण्डित भंगे।
भीष्मजननि मुनिवरकन्ये
पतित निवारिणि त्रिभुवन धन्ये॥
Praise of Maa Ganga as purifier and savior of the fallen. O Ganga, mother of Bhishma, redeemer of the sinful, blessed across the three worlds.

Our Story

About Ramkrishnapur Ghat रामकृष्णापुर घाट के बारे में

Nestled on the sacred banks of the Hooghly River in Howrah, Ramkrishnapur Ghat is a place where the divine and the mortal converge every evening in an atmosphere of profound devotion, ancient Vedic rituals, and timeless spirituality.

🏛

Sacred Location

Ramkrishnapur Ghat is situated on the western bank of the Hooghly River in Howrah, West Bengal — offering a serene riverfront setting for daily worship, spiritual gatherings, and community celebrations.

🌊

Significance of Maa Ganga

The River Ganga, known as Hooghly in this region, holds immense religious importance in Hindu tradition. Bathing in her waters is believed to cleanse sins and grant moksha — liberation from the cycle of birth and death.

🪔

Evening Aarti Tradition

Every evening as the sun sets over the Hooghly, the ghat comes alive with melodious temple bells, conch shells, and Vedic chants. Priests perform the sacred Ganga Aarti with large lamps, incense, flowers, and deep devotion.

🎶

Cultural Heart of Howrah

This ghat serves as a spiritual and cultural hub for the people of Howrah, hosting major festivals like Chhath Puja, Ganga Dussehra, Kartik Purnima, and various religious gatherings throughout the year.

Ganga Aarti — Full Lyrics गंगा आरती — सम्पूर्ण पाठ

Yeh hai Maa Ganga ki sampurn aarti — shuru se aakhir tak. Har sham 6:30 baje Ramkrishnapur Ghat par gayi jaati hai.

🎵 Main Dhun — Refrain (har pankti ke baad doharaaen)

जो नर तुमको ध्याता, मन वांछित फल पाता॥

Your radiance is like the moon, your water is pure and holy. Whoever takes refuge in you, they cross the ocean of life.
